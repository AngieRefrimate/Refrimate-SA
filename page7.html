ABOUT US
Refrimate was established in 2010.

We are an independent refrigeration spares wholesaler and retail company in South Africa and also in Zambia.


WE SPECIALISE IN COMMERCIAL & DOMESTIC REFRIGERATION SPARES

Refrimate has the exclusive distribution rights to Azak Condenser's & Evaporator , Huayi Compressors,Invotech Compressor's, Refricomp Compressors, Hongsen Tools, Rexnord and Dunli fan's.

We offer the widest range of quality products to our clients at the best prices for all your Domestic and Commercial refrigeration needs.
